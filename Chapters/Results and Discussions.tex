\section{Results and Discussions}

After extensive analysis of different combinations of satellite deployments and orbital altitude, the proposed mission shall be deployed at an SSO orbit at the highest altitude compliant to debris mitigation guidelines. For our scenarios this was set at 600 km. 
A minimum of 8 satellites are to be operational to fulfil the temporal resolution requirement, which proved to be the mission driver (as the spatial resolution was feasible within a 3U CubeSat).

Several deployment scenarios where analysed (see Table 5 18). The minimum number of CubeSats required to fulfil the mission always with 8 operational satellites is 24. Yet scenario 6, with a new batch of 4 satellites every 6 months starting at month 36 (and an initial batch of 8) was able to reduce the total number of CubeSats to 20 (with some periods where there are less than 8 satellites due to retirement).
